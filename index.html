About Me

Hello! I'm Zhao Zimo, currently a first-year PhD student in Data Science at the City University of Hong Kong (CityU), advised by Prof. Xiangyu Zhao. Prior to this, I earned my Master's degree in Data Science from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, where I was supervised by Prof. Benyou Wang and Prof. Xiao Li. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Data Science (with a minor in Finance) from Lingnan University, under the supervision of Prof. Haoran Xie.
My research interests lie in large language models, recommendation systems, and urban computing.
