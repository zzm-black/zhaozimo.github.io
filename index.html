Brief Biography

Zimo Zhao is currently a first-year PhD student in Data Science at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), advised by Prof. Xiangyu Zhao. He completed his Master's degree in Data Science at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, under the supervision of Prof. Benyou Wang and Prof. Xiao Li. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Data Science with a minor in Finance from Lingnan University, where he was supervised by Prof. Haoran Xie.

Research Interests

  • Model Compression and Accelerated Inference
  • Autonomous Learning and Self-Evolving Models
  • AI for Social Good: Healthcare, Finance, and Sustainability

His research has been published in top-tier venues including NeurIPS, International Journal of Low-Carbon Technologies, and Nicotine & Tobacco Research. He has been awarded Gold Medals at iENA International Trade Fair (2022) and Nuremberg International Competition Invention Award (2016). His work focuses on developing efficient and practical AI systems that can make a real-world impact.

He has worked on various cutting-edge projects including LLM memory optimization with quantized models, multi-agent systems for social simulation, and deep learning solutions for computer vision applications. His algorithms and models have been applied in real-world scenarios in collaboration with industry partners.
